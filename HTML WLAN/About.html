About Page

The goal of this Project is to Create an Internal Training site for WLAN Employees and an amazing resource for the WLAN Consortium.

The basic idea is to make training easier for recently hired employees by organizing everything into one place.
This site isn't limited to just the newly hired, but for seasoned employees as well.

Most of this information comes from the abundant resources locally on the WLAN Fox Group.
I wanted to set out and condense the majority of information to make it easy to learn as well as represent it visually,
that way it would effectively guide peers through what they need to know
to become successful quickly in the WLAN Consortium.

This will be designed on the premise that peers have no prior knowledge to anything wireless related.
This project will also be used as a leaning opportunity for the developer to dive right into the world of HTML, CSS, and a tiny bit of Javascript!
With both of these objectives in mind, hopefully this site can effectivly be used to meet both of these goals.


There's sensitive information displayed on parts of this site that's NOT For the eyes of the public,
it shall stay internally here at the IOL.
Thank you!
